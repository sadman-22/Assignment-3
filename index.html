Frequently ask question

🐶 Best dog food?

High-quality kibble or raw diet with protein, healthy fats, and essential vitamins. Brands like Orijen, Royal Canin, and Hill’s Science Diet are good choices.

🐱 How often to feed a cat?

2 times a day for adults, 3-4 times for kittens. Always provide fresh water.

🐰 Can rabbits eat carrots?

Yes, but in moderation. Too much can cause digestive issues due to high sugar content.

🐦 How long do parrots live?

Small parrots (budgies) live 5-10 years, while larger ones (macaws) can live 50+ years!

🐹 Can hamsters eat bananas?

Yes, but only as an occasional treat due to high sugar content. Got more pet questions? Ask away! 🐾😊
